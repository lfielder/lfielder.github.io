AI Ticket Resolution

Type: Operations Focus: Automation & Compliance

Approach

The AI Ticket Resolution tool accelerates problem-solving by giving employees immediate context, surfacing relevant historical data, and recommending secure and compliant solutions.

The system understands user roles and permissions, ensuring responses adhere to security governance while reducing resolution time and improving accuracy.

Outcomes

  • Improve response and resolution times
  • Decrease onboarding time for new employees
  • Recognize and capitalize on training opportunities

Next Steps

  1. Demo POC to leaders and users
  2. Pilot beta version with ~10 users
  3. Rollout full version to all employees
