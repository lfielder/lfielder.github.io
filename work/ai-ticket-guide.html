AI Ticket Guide

Type: Internal Tool Focus: Workflow Efficiency

The Problem

Design a natural-language-driven ticket creation experience that guides customers to accurately provide required details—such as classification and severity—reducing guesswork and improving ticket quality from the start.

The Solution

The AI Ticket Guide uses real-time AI prompting to guide users through the right questions and structure. It detects missing details, recommends categorization, and ensures clarity before submission.

Key Impact

  • Reduced back-and-forth communication
  • Improved workflow efficiency for support teams
  • Higher quality inputs and better data categorization
  • More intuitive process for non-technical users

Next Steps

  1. Usability test with users
  2. Allow users to opt-in to identify interest and get real-world feedback and analytics
  3. Rollout full version
